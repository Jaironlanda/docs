## Change Region

<Warning>Make sure there are no active requests from the application when changing the region. Otherwise, you will receive a `404` error once the region is updated.</Warning>
<Warning>Once the region is updated, please make sure you use the correct base URL for the selected region.</Warning>

To change the region, go to **Dashboard** > **[Select Silo]** > scroll to the bottom, and in the *Settings* section, click the `Manage` button to update your silo region.
