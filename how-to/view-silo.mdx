### Basic details

The image below shows basic information about the silo.

<img className="rounded" height="200" noZoom src="/images/view-silo/basic-detail.png" />

Based on the example above:

- **mydb**: This is your silo name. To edit, click the `...` button and select **Rename Silo** from the menu.
- **Private**: This is your silo access type.
- **Germany**: This is the region name where your silo is hosted.

### Endpoint

<img className="rounded" height="200" noZoom src="/images/view-silo/basic-detail-2.png" />

- **Endpoint**: Full endpoint name. This endpoint will change if you move to another available region.
- **File UUID**: Unique ID for your silo. This is unchangeable.
- **Last Update**: Date and time when you perform an update to your silo.
- **URL**: URL to access your silo. Please note that this will update if you change the silo access type.

### JSON file

<img className="rounded" height="200" noZoom src="/images/view-silo/basic-detail-3.png" />

To view or update your deployed JSON file, click the `JSON Editor` button. This will bring you to the [JSONsilo editor](/how-to/update-silo).
