## Introduction

This page is a guide on how to delete a silo through the user console.

## Delete silo

<Warning>Once Silo is deleted, there is no going back. Please be certain.</Warning>

To delete silo, go to **Dashboard** > **[Select Silo]** > scroll to the bottom, and in the *Settings > Danger Zone* section, click the `Delete` button to delete your silo.

<img className="rounded" height="200" noZoom src="/images/manage-silo/delete.png" />

When you click the `Delete` buttom, a confirmation message will appear, asking you to re-enter the silo name confirm the deletion of the silo.
